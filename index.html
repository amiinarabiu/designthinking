Overview

The Design Thinking Development Group comprises two subsidiaries that are both advancing innovative and evidence-based research and interventions in Nigeria and beyond. We work with communities, government, partners, and donors to improve social determinants of health using the principles of design thinking and responsive feedback.

Our Story

Inspired by the learnings from the COVID-19 responses, this group was formed and formalized in 2020 with the aim of making social and health interventions human-centered and responsive to feedback. The COVID-19 pandemic confirmed that the best results for complex problems are achieved when problems are framed in human-centric ways and solutions are implemented iteratively as dictated by feedback while interventions are live. We are poised to pursue research and interventions across our key focus areas – social determinants of health, women and children’s issues, health systems, infectious and chronic diseases, innovations, research, and advocacy – through innovative and human- centered approaches reinforced through real-time qualitative and quantitative feedback to improve the lives of the people as well as their environment around the world.

The two subsidiaries of the Design Thinking Group are;
1. Design Thinking initiative for Development – DTiD
2. Design Thinking Health Limited – DTHL

Our Focus Areas

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to improve social determinants of health of disadvantaged persons and communities through bold approaches that are innovative, evidence-based, feedback-driven, and sustainable. We understand the intricate linkage between people and their lived environment and the need to test and adapt solutions to problems using the principles of design thinking for maximum impact and sustainability.

OUR VISON

Our vision is to become the most reliable evidence and feedback-driven group organization delivering impactful and cost-effective social, health, and economic interventions among the vulnerable and marginalized settings around the world.

OUR CORE VALUES

We are relentless in our pursuit of quality and innovative ventures through partnership and intellectual inquiry of evidence. We are guided by the principles of honesty, respect, and accountability to our beneficiaries, partners, and stakeholders. We are prudent with resources while aiming for maximum impact and sustainability.
