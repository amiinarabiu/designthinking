About Us

The Design Thinking Development Group.

Who we are

The Design Thinking Development Group comprises two subsidiaries that are both advancing innovative and evidence-based research and interventions in Nigeria and beyond. We work with communities, government, partners, and donors to improve social determinants of health using the principles of design thinking and responsive feedback.Inspired by the learnings from the COVID-19 responses, this group was formed and formalized in 2020 with the aim of making social and health interventions human-centered and responsive to feedback. The COVID-19 pandemic confirmed that the best results for complex problems are achieved when problems are framed in human-centric ways and solutions are implemented iteratively as dictated by feedback while interventions are live. We are poised to pursue research and interventions across our key focus areas – social determinants of health, women and children’s issues, health systems, infectious and chronic diseases, innovations, research, and advocacy – through innovative and human- centered approaches reinforced through real-time qualitative and quantitative feedback to improve the lives of the people as well as their environment around the world.
